About Us

Taiping Jia, O.M.D. L.Ac, Dipl. Ac, C.H.

Taiping Jia, a NCCAOM certified acupuncturist.

I have over 20 years experience in acupuncture and traditional Chinese medicine

You can reach me at taiping@tjacupuncture.com

Or by phone at 503-475-9657


Education

I graduated in 1985 from the Beijing University of Chinese Medicine, the most reputable Chinese medicine school in China. I have practiced Chinese Medicine at Beijing Hospital, one of the prestigious and highly regarded hospitals in China. While I was there, I was the senior attending physician of integrative (Chinese and Western) Medicine. During this time I specialized in internal medicine, especially digestive diseases, pulmonary diseases, endocrine and metabolic disorders.


As a NCCAOM certified and experienced acupuncturist and Chinese Herbologist, I am ready and excited to help you regain or maintain your health.

Previous Experience

In addition to my work above, I have also worked at OCOM (Oregon College of Oriental Medicine) as a clinical supervisor.

I have also done extensive medical research for the University of Virginia and OHSU.
